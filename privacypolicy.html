Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Genesis collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

Information Collection

Genesis collects information necessary to provide its services. This includes Discord User IDs, Server IDs, and role information. When you use specific features like Leveling or Logging, we store message timestamps and activity metadata. We do not store the actual content of your messages unless specifically required for a configured feature (e.g., Starboard or Snipe).

How We Use Information

The data collected is used solely to maintain and improve the Bot's functionality. This includes managing leveling ranks, enforcing moderation rules, and providing personalized settings via our web dashboard. We do not sell or trade your personal data to third parties.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information. Your data is stored on secure servers with restricted access. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Cookies and Tracking

Our dashboard uses cookies to authenticate your Discord login and remember your preferences. These cookies are essential for the dashboard's operation. We may also use third-party analytics to monitor website traffic and improve user experience.

Data Deletion and Rights

Users have the right to request the deletion of their data. You can clear your leveling data or server settings using in-bot commands. For a full data wipe from our database, you may contact our support team. Please note that removing the Bot from a server does not automatically delete stored configuration data.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as payment processors (for Premium) and hosting providers. These services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reading them. Genesis is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party platforms like Discord or PayPal.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Compliance with Discord

Genesis operates in strict compliance with the Discord Developer Terms of Service and Developer Policy. We only access data that is granted via the OAuth2 scopes and Bot intents explicitly enabled by the server administrator.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the bottom of our documentation.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please join our official Support Server or reach out to the developers directly via the contact methods listed on the Genesis Dashboard.
